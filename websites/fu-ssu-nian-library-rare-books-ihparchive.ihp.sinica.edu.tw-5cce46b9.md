# Fu Ssu-nian Library Rare Books 傅斯年圖書館善本古籍數位典藏

**URL:** http://ihparchive.ihp.sinica.edu.tw/ihpkmc/ihpkm

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A digital archive of rare books and ancient Chinese manuscripts from the Fu Ssu-nian Library at Academia Sinica's Institute of History and Philology (IHP) in Taiwan. Named after Fu Ssu-nian, a prominent historian and founder of the IHP, the library holds one of the world's most important collections of Chinese rare books, including Song and Yuan dynasty editions, manuscripts, and woodblock prints. This digitization project provides online access to precious materials that span classical Chinese literature, history, philosophy, and collectanea (congshu). The digital archive preserves these cultural treasures while making them accessible to scholars worldwide for research in traditional Chinese bibliography, textual criticism, and classical studies.

## 摘要

中央研究院歷史語言研究所傅斯年圖書館珍貴圖書和中國古籍手稿的數位檔案。圖書館以著名歷史學家兼史語所創辦人傅斯年命名，擁有世界上最重要的中國善本收藏之一，包括宋元版本、手稿和木刻印刷品。此數位化專案提供對涵蓋中國古典文學、歷史、哲學和叢書的珍貴材料線上存取。數位檔案在保存這些文化瑰寶的同時，使全球學者能夠進行中國傳統書目學、校勘學和古典研究。
