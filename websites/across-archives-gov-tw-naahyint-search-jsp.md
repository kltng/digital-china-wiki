# Archives Cross-Boundaries（國家發展委員會檔案管理局Across檔案資源整合查詢平台）

**URL:** https://across.archives.gov.tw/naahyint/search.jsp

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

Archives Cross-Boundaries is Taiwan's integrated archival resource search platform managed by the National Development Council's Archives Administration (國家發展委員會檔案管理局). The platform provides cross-agency search capabilities for archival materials held across multiple Taiwanese government archives and institutions, enabling researchers to locate and access historical records from different archival collections through a unified interface. The system facilitates discovery of documents related to Taiwan's administrative history, government operations, and policy development. Users can search across archival databases and retrieve information about records held by various Taiwanese government agencies and cultural institutions.

## 摘要

Across 檔案資源整合查詢平台是由國家發展委員會檔案管理局管理的台灣整合性檔案資源搜尋平台。該平台提供跨機關檔案資料搜尋功能，讓研究人員可透過統一介面定位並存取台灣多個檔案館及機構所藏的歷史記錄。系統促進了與台灣行政史、政府運作及政策發展相關文獻的發現。使用者可跨檔案資料庫進行搜尋，並檢索台灣各政府機關及文化機構所藏檔案的相關資訊。
