# Dr. NH Online

**URL:** http://ahonline.drnh.gov.tw/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

An online digital archive platform for historical materials related to Taiwan, developed and maintained by the Taiwan government's archives administration. The platform provides access to digitized historical documents, government records, and archival materials documenting Taiwan's political and administrative development. Users can search through the digital collections to locate specific documents or browse by category and time period. The archive serves researchers, students, and the public interested in Taiwan's modern history, providing access to primary sources for understanding governmental policies, political transitions, and administrative practices.

## 摘要

台灣政府檔案管理局建置的數位檔案平台，收錄了與台灣歷史相關的史料。該平台提供對數位化歷史文件、政府記錄及檔案材料的線上存取，記錄了台灣的政治與行政發展歷程。使用者可搜尋數位典藏以定位特定文件，或按類別與時間瀏覽。此檔案館服務研究人員、學生及對台灣近現代史感興趣的大眾，提供理解政府政策、政治轉變及行政實踐的一手史料存取。
