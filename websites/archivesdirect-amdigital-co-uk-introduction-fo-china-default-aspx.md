# Archives Direct: Foreign Office Files for China (Adam Matthew Digital)

**URL:** https://www.archivesdirect.amdigital.co.uk/Introduction/FO_China/Default.aspx

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Archives Direct: Foreign Office Files for China is a digital archive collection from Adam Matthew Digital containing British diplomatic records concerning China from the early 20th century through the mid-1970s. The collection comprises correspondence, dispatches, reports, memoranda, and other official documents generated by the British Foreign Office and its diplomatic missions in China. These primary sources document British perspectives and policies regarding major events in modern Chinese history, including the establishment of the Republic of China, the Second Sino-Japanese War, the Chinese Civil War, and the establishment of the People's Republic of China. The archive provides valuable insights into Sino-British relations, international diplomacy, and Western understanding of Chinese political developments. The collection is fully searchable and includes digitized images of original documents with accompanying metadata.

## 摘要

Archives Direct: China Foreign Office Files 是 Adam Matthew Digital 的數位檔案收藏，包含從20世紀初至1970年代中期英國關於中國的外交記錄。該收藏由英國外交部及其駐華外交使團產生的通信、報告、備忘錄及其他官方文件組成。這些一手資料記錄了英國對現代中國歷史重大事件的觀點與政策，包括中華民國的建立、中日戰爭、國共內戰及中華人民共和國的建立。檔案館提供對中英關係、國際外交及西方對中國政治發展理解的寶貴見解。該收藏可完全搜尋，並包含原始文件的數位化影像及伴隨的元資料。
