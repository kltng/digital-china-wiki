# Ming Qing Women's Writings

**URL:** https://mhdb.mh.sinica.edu.tw/mingqing/mqww/

## Summary

The Ming Qing Women's Writings digital archive is a specialized database dedicated to preserving and providing access to writings by women during the Ming and Qing dynasties in China. Originally hosted at McGill University, the archive has migrated to Academia Sinica and is accessible through the Ming History Database (MHDB). The database serves as an invaluable resource for scholars studying Chinese literary history, women's history, and gender studies, offering primary source materials that document women's literary production, intellectual activities, and social roles during these historical periods. The archive continues to be maintained and is expected to announce a new URL after the migration is complete.

## 摘要

明清婦女著作數位典藏是專門保存與提供明清時期中國婦女著作線上存取的專業資料庫。該資料庫最初由麥吉爾大學託管，現已遷移至中央研究院，並可透過明史資料庫（MHDB）存取。資料庫是研究中國文學史、婦女史與性別研究的寶貴資源，提供一手史料，記錄這些歷史時期婦女的文學創作、學術活動與社會角色。該資料庫持續維護中，預計在遷移完成後公告新網址。
