# Chinese Loans to Africa Database (open access)

**URL:** https://www.bu.edu/gdp/chinese-loans-to-africa-database

**Region:** Global

## Summary

The Chinese Loans to Africa (CLA) Database is an interactive data project tracking loan commitments from Chinese development finance institutions (DFIs), commercial banks, government entities and companies to African governments, state-owned enterprises and regional multilateral institutions.
