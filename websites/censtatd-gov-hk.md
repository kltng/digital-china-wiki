# (香港)政府統計處 (Hong Kong) Census and Statistics Department

**URL:** http://www.censtatd.gov.hk/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The official statistics agency of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region government, responsible for collecting, compiling, and disseminating statistical data on Hong Kong's economy, society, and population. The Census and Statistics Department provides comprehensive datasets including population censuses, economic indicators, trade statistics, labor market data, household surveys, and various thematic statistical reports. Key statistics cover population size and structure, unemployment rates, GDP, consumer price indices, retail sales, and business sector performance. The website offers online data access, statistical publications, and historical time series data, making it a primary source for quantitative research on Hong Kong.

## 摘要

香港特別行政區政府負責收集、編製和發布香港經濟、社會及人口統計數據的官方統計機構。政府統計處提供全面的數據集，包括人口普查、經濟指標、貿易統計、勞動市場數據、住戶調查及各類主題統計報告。主要統計數據涵蓋人口數量與結構、失業率、本地生產總值、消費物價指數、零售銷售額及商業部門表現等。該網站提供線上數據存取、統計刊物和歷史時間序列數據，是進行香港定量研究的主要來源。
