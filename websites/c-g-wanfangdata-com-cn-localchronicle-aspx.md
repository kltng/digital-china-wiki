# Wanfang Data: China Local Gazetteers Database (万方数据: 新方志)

**URL:** http://c.g.wanfangdata.com.cn/LocalChronicle.aspx

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A specialized database within the Wanfang Data platform providing access to China's local gazetteers (地方志/方志), the official comprehensive local histories compiled by Chinese governments at provincial, municipal, and county levels. These contemporary "new gazetteers" (新方志) document local geography, history, economy, culture, politics, society, and notable figures, continuing a Chinese tradition of local historiography that spans over 2,000 years. The database serves as an invaluable resource for researchers studying Chinese regional development, local administration, economic geography, demographic changes, and comparative regional studies. Access typically requires institutional subscription through academic or research libraries.

## 摘要

萬方數據平台中的專門資料庫，提供中國地方志的存取，這是由中國省、市、縣各級政府編纂的官方全面地方歷史。這些當代「新方志」記錄地方地理、歷史、經濟、文化、政治、社會和著名人物，延續了中國長達兩千多年的地方修史傳統。該資料庫為研究中國區域發展、地方行政、經濟地理、人口變遷和比較區域研究的研究人員提供了無價值的資源。存取通常需要通過學術或研究圖書館的機構訂閱。
