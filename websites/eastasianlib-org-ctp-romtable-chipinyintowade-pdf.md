# Pinyin to Wade-Giles to Zhuyin Conversion Table

**URL:** https://www.eastasianlib.org/ctp/RomTable/Chipinyintowade.pdf

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

A comprehensive PDF conversion table providing mappings between three major Chinese romanization systems: Pinyin (拼音), Wade-Giles, and Zhuyin (注音, also known as Bopomofo). This reference tool is essential for researchers working with older publications and catalog records that use Wade-Giles romanization, which was the standard system in Western scholarship for many decades. The table enables quick conversion between systems, facilitating cross-referencing of Chinese names, places, and terms across different bibliographic sources and historical documents published before Pinyin became the international standard in the 1980s.

## 摘要

一份全面的PDF轉換表，提供三種主要中文羅馬拼音系統之間的對應關係：拼音、威妥瑪拼音和注音（也稱為注音符號）。此參考工具對於使用威妥瑪拼音系統的舊版出版物和目錄記錄的研究人員至關重要，該系統在數十年間是西方學術界的標準。該表格可快速進行不同系統間的轉換，便於在不同書目來源和1980年代拼音成為國際標準前出版的歷史文獻之間交叉參考中文姓名、地名和術語。
