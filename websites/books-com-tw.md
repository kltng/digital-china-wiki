# Books.com.tw (讀冊生活) - Taiwanese Online Bookstore

**URL:** https://www.books.com.tw/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Books.com.tw (讀冊生活), formerly known as Boqun (博書網), is Taiwan's leading online bookstore and book retail platform. The website offers an extensive catalog of books published in Taiwan, including literature, academic works, textbooks, professional publications, and translated works across all subject areas. Books.com.tw provides detailed bibliographic information, author profiles, reader reviews, and promotional pricing. The platform features editorial recommendations, bestseller lists, and special collections curated by booksellers. It serves as an important resource for identifying and acquiring Taiwanese publications, with search capabilities by title, author, publisher, ISBN, and category. The site also operates a physical bookstore network and offers membership services with loyalty benefits. For researchers, it is a valuable tool for tracking contemporary Taiwanese publishing trends and accessing reader reviews.
