# Hong Kong Journals Online (HKJO) 香港期刊在線 OPEN ACCESS

**URL:** https://hkjo.lib.hku.hk/exhibits/show/hkjo/home

**Language:** Chinese

**Region:** China

## Summary

HKUL>Digital Initiatives> Hong Kong Journals Online Hong Kong Journals Online (HKJO) is a full-text image database providing access to selected academic and professional journals, both in English and Chinese, published in Hong Kong. Titles included in this database cover a wide range of disciplines including law, medicine and education. The University of Hong Kong Libraries, with a rich collection of Hong Kong journals, developed this database with the objectives of facilitating efficient information retrieval and preserving archival materials.
