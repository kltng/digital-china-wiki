# Gale. China and the Modern World: Records of the Maritime Customs Service of China 1854–1949

**URL:** https://go.gale.com/ps/dispBasicSearch.do?userGroupName=camb55135

**Language:** English

**Region:** Global

## Summary

Gale's "China and the Modern World" series provides digitized archival records from the 19th and 20th centuries documenting China's relations with the Western world. The collection includes diplomatic correspondence, missionary records, trade documents, and government papers covering topics such as the Opium Wars, the Treaty Ports, the Boxer Rebellion, and Republican-era China.
