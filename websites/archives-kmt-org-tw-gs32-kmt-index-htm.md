# Kuomintang Party Archives System 中國國民黨黨史館館藏檔案目錄檢索系統

**URL:** http://archives.kmt.org.tw/gs32/kmt/index.htm?cache=1400701295256

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The Kuomintang (Guomindang) Party Archives System is the official archival repository of the Kuomintang political party, housed at the Party History Museum in Taiwan. The database provides searchable access to historical documents, records, and materials spanning the party's history from its founding in the late 19th century through various periods of Chinese and Taiwanese history. The collection includes organizational records, meeting minutes, correspondence, policy documents, photographs, and other archival materials. Researchers can search the catalog by various criteria to locate relevant materials. The archives serve as an important primary source for studying the history of the Kuomindang, its role in Republican China, and its activities in Taiwan after 1949. Access policies and restrictions may apply to certain materials in the collection.

## 摘要

中國國民黨黨史館館藏檔案目錄檢索系統是國民黨政黨的官方檔案庫，設置於台灣的黨史館。該資料庫提供對歷史文件、記錄及資料的可搜尋存取，涵蓋國民黨自19世紀末成立以來各時期的歷史。收藏包括組織記錄、會議紀錄、通信、政策文件、照片及其他檔案材料。研究者可使用各種條件搜尋目錄以尋找相關材料。該檔案館是研究國民黨歷史、其在民國時期的角色，以及1949年後在台灣活動的重要一手史料來源。部分收藏材料可能適用存取政策與限制。
