# CNKI Overseas Book Database

**URL:** http://book.oversea.cnki.net/ccgbweb/book/index

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The CNKI Overseas Book Database is an online platform providing access to Chinese academic books and scholarly monographs through the China National Knowledge Infrastructure (CNKI) network. The database features full-text or chapter-level access to thousands of Chinese-language books covering various academic disciplines including humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and engineering. Content includes academic monographs, professional reference works, textbooks, and conference proceedings published in China. The platform offers powerful search capabilities by title, author, publisher, ISBN, and subject classification, with advanced filtering options. Users can view book details, tables of contents, and full text where available. The service is designed primarily for overseas institutions and researchers who need access to Chinese scholarly publications, offering institutional subscription access with IP authentication or individual login options.

## 摘要

CNKI海外圖書資料庫是透過中國知網（CNKI）網絡提供中文学術書籍及學術專著存取的線上平台。該資料庫收錄數千種中文書籍的全文或章節級別存取，涵蓋人文學科、社會科學、自然科學及工程等各學術領域。內容包括在中國出版的學術專著、專業參考著作、教科書及會議論文集。該平台提供按書名、作者、出版社、ISBN及學科分類的強大搜尋功能，具進階篩選選項。使用者可檢視書籍詳細資訊、目錄及全文（如有）。該服務主要為需要存取中文學術出版品的海外機構及研究人員設計，提供IP驗證或個人登入選項的機構訂閱存取。
