# Airiti Chinese Electronic Proceedings Services 華藝中文會議論文資料庫

**URL:** http://nrs.harvard.edu/urn-3:hul.eresource:airitilibrary

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A Chinese academic database providing electronic access to conference proceedings (會議論文) and papers published by Taiwanese and Chinese scholars and institutions. The platform collects conference papers from academic meetings, symposia, and professional conferences across various disciplines, offering full-text search capabilities and digital document access. The database serves researchers seeking the latest research findings presented at academic conferences, particularly in fields related to Chinese studies, humanities, social sciences, and interdisciplinary research. Through institutional subscription arrangements, libraries can provide access to users for searching and downloading conference proceedings in Chinese language.

## 摘要

提供會議論文數位存取的學術資料庫，收錄台灣及中國學者與機構發表的會議論文。平台收集各學術研討會、座談會及專業會議的會議論文，提供全文搜尋功能與數位文件存取。該資料庫服務尋找學術會議發表最新研究成果的研究人員，特別是在漢學、人文、社會科學及跨領域研究方面。透過機構授權安排，圖書館可為使用者提供搜尋及下載中文會議論文的服務。
