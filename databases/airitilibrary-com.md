# Airiti Library 華藝台灣電子期刊

**URL:** https://www.airitilibrary.com/

**Language:** Chinese

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Airiti Library is a Chinese-language electronic journal and academic article database focusing on publications from Taiwan. The platform provides access to scholarly journals, periodicals, and academic articles in Chinese, supporting research across humanities, social sciences, and other disciplines. Users can search for specific journals, browse by subject category, or search for articles across the collection. The database serves researchers, students, and scholars seeking Chinese-language academic literature from Taiwanese publishers and institutions.

## 摘要

Airiti Library 是以台灣出版品為主的中文電子期刊與學術論文資料庫。平台提供人文、社會科學及其他學科中文學術期刊、定期刊物及學術文章的存取。使用者可搜尋特定期刊、按學科類別瀏覽，或跨收藏搜尋文章。該資料庫服務尋求台灣出版社及機構中文學術文獻的研究人員、學生與學者。
