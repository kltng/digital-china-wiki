# Apabi China Digital Library (Not Available)

**URL:** not_available_nrs.harvard.edu

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The Apabi China Digital Library was a comprehensive Chinese digital resource platform providing access to e-books, journals, and reference works in Chinese language. The database offered scholarly publications across disciplines including humanities, social sciences, and technology, supporting research in Chinese studies. While the platform was previously accessible through institutional subscriptions, the current link indicates the service is no longer available through Harvard's eresources. The Apabi collection included valuable Chinese academic content for researchers requiring Chinese-language digital materials.

## 摘要

Apabi China Digital Library 曾是綜合性的中文數位資源平台，提供中文電子書、期刊及參考工具書的存取。該資料庫收錄人文、社會科學及科技等學科的學術出版品，支持漢學研究。該平台曾透過機構訂閱提供服務，目前連結顯示哈佛電子資源已無法存取此服務。Apabi 收藏包含對需要中文數位材料的研究人員而言極具價值的中文學術內容。
