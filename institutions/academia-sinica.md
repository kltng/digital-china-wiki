# Academia Sinica 中央研究院

Taiwan's preeminent academic research institution, founded in 1928. Academia Sinica operates numerous digital humanities projects through its institutes, particularly the Institute of History and Philology, Institute of Taiwan History, and Research Center for Digital Humanities.
