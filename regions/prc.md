# People's Republic of China 中华人民共和国

Digital resources for the study of mainland China, covering the PRC period (1949-present) as well as imperial and republican era materials held in Chinese institutions. The PRC hosts extensive digitization programs through institutions like the National Library of China, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, and various university libraries.

## Major Institutions

- National Library of China
- Chinese Academy of Social Sciences
- Peking University Library
- Shanghai Library
- Fudan University Library
