# Japan 日本

Japanese institutions hold extensive collections of Chinese materials, including rare books, manuscripts, and historical documents acquired over centuries of cultural exchange. Key collections include the National Diet Library, Toyo Bunko, and university East Asian libraries.

## Major Institutions

- National Diet Library
- Toyo Bunko
- University of Tokyo
- Kyoto University
- Tenri Library
