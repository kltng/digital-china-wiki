# Taiwan 台湾

Digital resources for the study of Taiwan, covering the island's history from indigenous peoples through Dutch/Spanish colonization, Qing administration, Japanese rule (1895-1945), and the post-war Republic of China period. Taiwan is a world leader in Chinese-language digital humanities, with Academia Sinica, the National Central Library, and the National Palace Museum hosting major digital collections.

## Major Institutions

- Academia Sinica
- National Central Library
- National Palace Museum
- Institute of Taiwan History
- Taiwan Historical Records Office
