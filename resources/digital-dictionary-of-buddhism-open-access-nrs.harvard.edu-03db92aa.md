# Digital Dictionary of Buddhism 電子佛教辭典 OPEN ACCESS

**URL:** http://nrs.harvard.edu/urn-3:hul.eresource:ddicbudd

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

An authoritative digital reference work providing comprehensive coverage of Buddhist terminology, concepts, figures, and texts. The dictionary includes entries on Sanskrit, Pali, Tibetan, Chinese, and Japanese Buddhist terminology, with explanations of doctrinal concepts, biographical information on important Buddhist figures, and descriptions of Buddhist texts and schools. This resource is essential for researchers studying Buddhism in China and East Asia, Buddhist philosophy, and the translation and interpretation of Buddhist scriptures. The open access version makes this specialized scholarly reference work freely available to students and scholars worldwide.

## 摘要

權威數位參考著作，提供佛教術語、概念、人物和文本的全面覆蓋。該辭典包括梵文、巴利文、藏文、中文和日文佛教術語的條目，包含對教義概念的解釋、重要佛教人物的傳記資訊以及佛教文本和學派的描述。該資源對研究中國和東亞佛教、佛教哲學以及佛教經典翻譯和解讀的研究人員至關重要。開放取用版本使這部專門學術參考著作向全球學生和學者免費開放。
