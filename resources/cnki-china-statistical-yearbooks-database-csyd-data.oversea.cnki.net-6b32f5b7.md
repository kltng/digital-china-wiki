# CNKI. China Statistical Yearbooks Database (CSYD) 中国经济社会大数据平台

**URL:** https://data.oversea.cnki.net/

**Language:** Chinese

**Region:** China

## Summary

CNKI (China National Knowledge Infrastructure) is the largest academic database in China, providing access to journal articles, dissertations, conference papers, newspapers, and reference works across all academic disciplines. The overseas portal provides English-language access to Chinese scholarly content.
