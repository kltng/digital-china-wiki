# General policies

**URL:** https://guides.library.stanford.edu/general-policies

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A library guide from Stanford University outlining general policies governing library access, borrowing privileges, and use of library services. This resource provides information on circulation policies, interlibrary loan services, remote access to electronic resources, library hours, and guidelines for using special collections. The policy guide helps researchers, students, and faculty understand the rules and procedures for accessing Stanford's extensive library collections, including materials relevant to Chinese and East Asian studies. This information is essential for effectively utilizing library resources for research and coursework.

## 摘要

史丹佛大學圖書館指南，概述管理圖書館存取、借閱權限和使用圖書館服務的一般政策。該資源提供有關流通政策、館際互借服務、電子資源遠端存取、圖書館開放時間和使用特藏指南的資訊。政策指南幫助研究人員、學生和教職員了解存取史丹佛廣泛圖書館收藏的規則和程序，包括與中文和東亞研究相關的材料。此資訊對於有效利用圖書館資源進行研究和課程學習至關重要。
