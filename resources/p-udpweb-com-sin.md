## Summary

A digital collection platform for Taiwan academic classics (台灣學術經典), providing access to important scholarly works in Chinese. The database features digitized versions of significant academic publications across humanities and social sciences disciplines, including history, literature, philosophy, and Taiwan studies. This resource serves researchers and students seeking foundational scholarly texts in Chinese, particularly those focused on Taiwan-related topics. The collection includes works by prominent scholars and represents important contributions to Chinese-language academic discourse on Taiwan and related subjects.
