# Digital Dictionary of Buddhism

**URL:** https://buddhism-dict.net/ddb/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

The Digital Dictionary of Buddhism (DDB) is an authoritative online reference work providing comprehensive information on Buddhist terms, concepts, figures, texts, and traditions. The dictionary encompasses Buddhist studies across various Asian traditions, with extensive coverage of Chinese Buddhism and related East Asian Buddhist developments. Entries include Sanskrit, Pali, Tibetan, Chinese, and Japanese terms with detailed explanations, textual references, and cross-references. Compiled by leading scholars in the field, the DDB serves as an essential resource for researchers studying Buddhist texts, philosophy, and history. The platform offers sophisticated search capabilities including full-text searching of entries, character lookups, and browsing by category. Content is regularly updated with new entries and revisions based on recent scholarship. The dictionary is particularly valuable for those working with Buddhist canonical texts and translations.
