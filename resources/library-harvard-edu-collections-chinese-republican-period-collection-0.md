# 哈佛燕京圖書館民國時期文獻 Harvard-Yenching Library Chinese Republican Period (1911-1949) Collection

**URL:** https://library.harvard.edu/collections/chinese-republican-period-collection-0

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A digitization project by Harvard-Yenching Library making accessible Chinese materials from the Republican period (1911-1949), a transformative era in modern Chinese history. The collection includes books, periodicals, manuscripts, and other printed materials documenting political, social, economic, and cultural developments during this period. Resources cover topics such as the May Fourth Movement, the rise of nationalism, the Warlord Era, the Sino-Japanese War, and the Chinese Civil War. This digital collection preserves rare and fragile materials while providing scholars worldwide access to primary sources for studying modern Chinese intellectual history, political thought, and social change.

## 摘要

哈佛燕京圖書館的數位化專案，提供中國民國時期（1911-1949年）資料的存取，這是現代中國歷史的轉型時代。該收藏包括書籍、期刊、手稿和其他印刷材料，記錄了這一時期的政治、社會、經濟和文化發展。資料涵蓋五四運動、民族主義興起、軍閥時期、中日戰爭和國共內戰等主題。該數位收藏在保存珍貴且易損毀材料的同时，為全球學者提供研究中國現代思想史、政治思想和社会變革的主要資料。
