# Digital Archive of Rare Books（善本古籍數位典藏系統）

**URL:** http://da.lib.ntnu.edu.tw/rarebook/ug-0.jsp

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A digital archive system for rare books (善本古籍) maintained by the National Taiwan Normal University Library (國立臺灣師範大學圖書館). This collection features digitized versions of rare Chinese classical texts and manuscripts, covering a wide range of subjects including literature, history, philosophy, and traditional culture. The digital archive provides high-resolution images and searchable metadata for valuable pre-modern Chinese books, facilitating scholarly access to important primary sources that would otherwise be difficult to consult due to rarity and preservation concerns. The system organizes materials by category and supports browsing and searching of the digitized collections.

## 摘要

國立臺灣師範大學圖書館維護的善本古籍數位典藏系統。此收藏收錄了稀有的中國古典文獻和手稿的數位化版本，內容涵蓋文學、歷史、哲學和傳統文化等廣泛領域。該數位典藏系統為珍貴的近代以前中文書籍提供高解析度影像和可搜尋的元數據，便利學者存取重要的原始資料，解決了因文獻稀有和保存需求造成的查閱困難。系統按類別組織資料，支援數位化收藏的瀏覽和搜尋功能。
