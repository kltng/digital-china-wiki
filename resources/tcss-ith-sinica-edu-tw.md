# Taiwan Documents Collection Database

**URL:** http://tcss.ith.sinica.edu.tw


## Summary

The Taiwan Documents Collection Database (臺灣文獻叢刊資料庫系統) is a specialized database maintained by the Institute of Taiwan History at Academia Sinica, dedicated to providing access to historical documents related to Taiwan. The database contains a comprehensive collection of primary source materials including historical records, government documents, personal writings, and other archival materials that document Taiwan's history from various periods. This digital resource serves scholars, researchers, and students studying Taiwan history by making rare and historically significant documents accessible online.

## 摘要

臺灣文獻叢刊資料庫系統是由中央研究院臺灣史研究所維護的專業資料庫，專門提供與臺灣相關的歷史文獻線上存取。該資料庫包含豐富的一手史料，涵蓋歷史記錄、政府檔案、個人著作以及其他檔案資料，記錄了臺灣各個時期的歷史。此數位資源為學者、研究人員和學生提供線上存取珍貴歷史文獻的管道，促進臺灣史學研究的發展。
