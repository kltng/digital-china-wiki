# China Survey Data Archive (CSDA) - Peking University Open Research Data Platform

**URL:** https://opendata.pku.edu.cn/dataverse/CSDA

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

The China Survey Data Archive (CSDA) is an open access research data repository hosted on the Peking University Open Research Data Platform. This archive provides systematic preservation and sharing of survey datasets related to China, supporting empirical research in social sciences. Major collections include the China Family Panel Studies (CFPS), Enterprise Survey for Innovation and Entrepreneurship in China, Panel Survey Data on business environment evaluation, and various cross-sectional survey datasets. The platform facilitates data discovery, downloading, and citation, with datasets covering topics such as demographic trends, household characteristics, business innovation, economic indicators, and social attitudes in contemporary China.

## 摘要

中國調查數據資料庫（CSDA）是託管於北京大學開放研究數據平台的開放取用研究數據庫。該資料庫提供與中國相關的調查數據集的系統保存與共享，支持社會科學實證研究。主要收藏包括中國家庭追蹤調查（CFPS）、中國企業創新與創業調查、營商環境評估面板調查數據，以及各種橫斷面調查數據集。該平台促進數據發現、下載和引用，數據集涵蓋當代中國的人口趨勢、住戶特徵、企業創新、經濟指標和社會態度等主題。
