# 國史館臺灣文獻館文獻檔案查詢系統

**URL:** https://onlinearchives.th.gov.tw/index.php?act=Archive%2Findex

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A document and archive search system maintained by the Taiwan Historica (國史館臺灣文獻館), the national archival institution responsible for collecting and preserving Taiwan's historical records. This online system provides searchable access to a vast collection of archival documents covering Taiwan's history from the Qing dynasty through Japanese colonial rule (1895-1945) to the post-war period. Materials include government documents, administrative records, official correspondence, land records, census data, and other primary sources documenting Taiwan's historical development. The database serves as a crucial resource for researchers studying Taiwan history, colonial studies, regional development, and cross-strait relations.

## 摘要

國史館臺灣文獻館維護的文獻和檔案查詢系統，該館是負責收集和保存臺灣歷史記錄的國家檔案機構。該線上系統提供對大量檔案文件的可搜尋存取，涵蓋臺灣從清朝、日本殖民統治（1895-1945年）到戰後時期的歷史。資料包括政府文件、行政記錄、官方通信、土地記錄、戶口普查數據以及記錄臺灣歷史發展的其他主要來源。該資料庫為研究臺灣歷史、殖民研究、區域發展和海峽兩岸關係的研究人員提供重要資源。
