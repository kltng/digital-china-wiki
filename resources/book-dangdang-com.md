# Dangdang.com (當當網) - Online Bookstore

**URL:** http://book.dangdang.com/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Dangdang.com (當當網) is one of China's largest online bookstores and e-commerce platforms, originally established as a book-focused retailer and later expanding into general merchandise. The books section offers millions of Chinese-language titles across all categories, including academic publications, literature, textbooks, children's books, and specialized works. The platform provides detailed bibliographic information, user reviews, and purchasing options for books published in mainland China and Taiwan. Dangdang serves as an important commercial resource for identifying and acquiring contemporary Chinese publications, with search capabilities by author, title, publisher, ISBN, and subject category. While primarily a commercial platform, it is widely used by libraries, scholars, and collectors for collection development and research on Chinese publishing trends. The site also offers e-books and audiobooks alongside physical books.

## 摘要

當當網是中國最大的線上書店及電子商務平台之一，最初成立為以書籍為重點的零售商，後擴展至一般商品。書籍區提供數百萬種跨越所有類別的中文書名，包括學術出版物、文學作品、教科書、兒童讀物及專業著作。該平台提供在中國大陸及台灣出版書籍的詳細書目資訊、使用者評論及購買選項。當當是識別及獲取當代中文出版品的重要商業資源，可按作者、書名、出版社、ISBN及主題類別搜尋。雖然主要為商業平台，但廣泛被圖書館、學者及收藏家用於館藏發展及中國出版趨勢研究。網站除了實體書籍外，也提供電子書及有聲書。
