# Douban Books (豆瓣读书)

**URL:** http://book.douban.com/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Douban Books (豆瓣读书) is the book section of Douban, a popular Chinese social networking and cultural platform that combines user reviews, recommendations, and social interaction around books, movies, and music. The platform features comprehensive bibliographic information for Chinese-language books published in mainland China, Taiwan, and Hong Kong, including user-submitted reviews, ratings, reading lists, and discussion forums. Users can track their reading, create bookshelves, and follow other readers with similar interests. Douban Books serves as an important resource for discovering contemporary Chinese publications, understanding readership reception, and accessing community-generated content about Chinese literature and academic works. The platform includes author profiles, publisher information, and social features that facilitate discussion of books among Chinese readers. It is widely used by researchers to study reading trends, popular literature, and cultural discourse in contemporary China.
