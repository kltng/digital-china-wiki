# Dictionary of East Asian Literary Terms (DEALT)

**URL:** https://buddhism-dict.net/dealt/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

The Dictionary of East Asian Literary Terms (DEALT) is a specialized online reference work focusing on literary terminology from classical Chinese and Japanese traditions. The dictionary provides explanations and translations of technical terms, concepts, and expressions used in pre-modern Chinese and Japanese literature, poetry, and literary criticism. It serves as a valuable tool for researchers and students working with classical texts, offering insights into literary devices, genres, and critical concepts. The dictionary includes original terms in Chinese and Japanese characters with English translations and scholarly explanations. The platform supports searching by term and browsing by categories. DEALT complements other digital dictionaries in the same platform's collection, providing focused coverage of literary terminology that is essential for understanding pre-modern East Asian literary traditions and textual analysis.

## 摘要

東亞文學術語辭典（DEALT）是一個專門的線上參考著作，聚焦於中國古典及日本傳統的文學術語。該辭典提供前現代中國及日本文學、詩歌及文學批評中使用之技術術語、概念及表達方式的解釋與翻譯。它是處理古典文本的研究者及學生的寶貴工具，提供對文學手法、文類及批評概念的見解。該辭典包含中文及日文字元形式的原始術語，附帶英文翻譯及學術解釋。該平台支持按術語搜尋及按類別瀏覽。DEALT 補充了同平台收藏中的其他數位辭典，提供理解前現代東亞文學傳統及文本分析所必需的文學術語專門涵蓋。
