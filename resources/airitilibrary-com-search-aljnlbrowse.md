# Airiti Library Journal Browse 華藝期刊瀏覽

**URL:** https://www.airitilibrary.com/search/aljnlbrowse

**Language:** Chinese

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Airiti Library's journal browse page is part of the broader Airiti academic platform, which the live site describes as including CEPS Chinese electronic journals, CETD theses and dissertations, conference papers, and integrated ebook discovery. This specific interface is designed for publication browsing rather than article search: the page exposes publication-name search, journal-oriented browse actions, and filtering logic for subject classification, journal indicators, region, and related publication metadata. It is most useful when you want to identify a journal title first and then navigate into that publication's information and issue-level records within Airiti.

## 摘要

此頁面是 Airiti Library 的期刊瀏覽介面，隸屬華藝線上圖書館整體學術平台。根據網站即時內容，Airiti Library 整合 CEPS 中文電子期刊、CETD 電子學位論文、研討會論文與電子書等資源；而這個網址主要用於「查詢出版品」與瀏覽期刊，而非直接檢索單篇文章。介面支援依出版品名稱、學科分類、期刊指標、地區等條件篩選，適合先鎖定期刊名稱，再進一步查看各刊資訊與卷期內容。
