# 康熙字典網上版 Kangxi Zidian

**URL:** http://www.kangxizidian.com/index2.php

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

An online digital version of the Kangxi Zidian (康熙字典, Kangxi Dictionary), the comprehensive Chinese character dictionary commissioned by the Kangxi Emperor and completed in 1716. This standard reference work contains approximately 47,000 characters, providing definitions, pronunciations, variants, and classical citations for Chinese characters. The dictionary remains an essential tool for reading classical Chinese texts, understanding traditional philology, and researching the development of Chinese characters over centuries. This digital version makes the authoritative reference work accessible online, facilitating research in classical Chinese studies, historical linguistics, and traditional textual scholarship.

## 摘要

《康熙字典》的線上數位版本，這是由康熙帝下令編纂並於1716年完成的中國漢字綜合字典。這部標準參考著作包含約47,000個漢字，提供漢字的定義、讀音、異體字和古籍引用。該字典仍是閱讀古典中文文本、理解傳統語言學和研究數百年來漢字發展的基本工具。此數位版本使這部權威參考著作可線上存取，便利古典中文研究、歷史語言學和傳統文本學研究。
