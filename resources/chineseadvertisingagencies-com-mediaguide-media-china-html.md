## Summary

Established in 1998, Chinese Advertising Agencies, Inc. has been engaged in providing information to companies and individuals who wish to advertise in Chinese media. Since 2002, we published Chinese Media Guide, an online guide that lists major overseas Chinese media including Chinese newspapers, Chinese TV stations, Chinese radios, and Chinese websites.
