# HathiTrust Digital Library

**URL:** https://hathitrust.org/digital_library

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A massive digital library partnership between major academic and research institutions, providing access to millions of digitized books, journals, and other materials. The HathiTrust Digital Library includes extensive collections in Chinese and other Asian languages, containing works on Chinese history, literature, philosophy, religion, and more. Many materials are in the public domain and freely accessible, while others are available through member institution login. The platform offers full-text search across the entire corpus, making it a valuable resource for locating historical Chinese texts, rare books, and scholarly works for research and study.

## 摘要

HathiTrust 數位圖書館是由主要學術和研究機構組成的夥伴關係，提供對數百萬部數位化書籍、期刊和其他資料的存取。該數位圖書館包含大量中文和其他亞洲語言的收藏，內容涉及中國歷史、文學、哲學、宗教等領域。許多資料屬於公共領域，可自由存取，其他資料則可透過會員機構登入使用。該平台提供對整個語料庫的全文搜尋功能，是尋找歷史中文文本、善本書籍和學術著作進行研究和學習的重要資源。
