# Ta Kung Pao 大公報, 1902-1949

**URL:** http://tk.dhcdb.com.tw/tknewsc/tknewskm

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Digitized archive of Ta Kung Pao (大公報), one of China's most influential and long-running newspapers, covering the period from 1902 to 1949. Founded in Tianjin, Ta Kung Pao played a significant role in modern Chinese journalism, known for its relatively progressive editorial stance and intellectual engagement during the Republican era. The newspaper provides extensive coverage of major historical events including the 1911 Revolution, the May Fourth Movement, the Warlord Era, the Sino-Japanese War, and the Chinese Civil War. This digital archive offers full-text searchable access to historical news reports, editorials, and cultural commentary, making it an essential primary source for researching early 20th-century Chinese history, politics, and intellectual thought.
