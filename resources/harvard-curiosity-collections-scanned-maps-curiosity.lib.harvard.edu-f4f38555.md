# Harvard Curiosity collections Scanned Maps

**URL:** https://curiosity.lib.harvard.edu/scanned-maps

**Region:** Global

## Summary

Thousands of maps and atlases from around the world are available online in hi-res from the Harvard Map Collection. These maps are only a small fraction of the complete holdings in the Harvard Map Collection. You can search for more maps that are not online in HOLLIS, the Harvard Library's online catalog. Thousands of maps an atlases covering all parts of the world have been scanned and are now available online.
