# EIU ViewPoint (Economist Intelligence Unit)

**URL:** https://viewpoint.eiu.com/analysis

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

An online platform from the Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) providing comprehensive country analysis, forecasts, and risk assessments. EIU ViewPoint offers in-depth coverage of economic trends, political developments, business environments, and regulatory landscapes for countries worldwide, including China. The platform features country reports, economic forecasts, political risk assessments, industry analysis, and special reports on specific topics such as China's economic reforms, foreign policy, and domestic challenges. This subscription-based resource serves businesses, policymakers, investors, and researchers seeking authoritative analysis and data-driven insights into China's economic and political trajectory.

## 摘要

經濟學人智庫（EIU）提供的線上平台，提供全面的國家分析、預測和風險評估。EIU ViewPoint提供對全球包括中國在內的經濟趨勢、政治發展、商業環境和監管格局的深入報導。該平台特色包括國家報告、經濟預測、政治風險評估、產業分析以及關於中國經濟改革、外交政策和國內挑戰等特定主題的專題報告。此訂閱制資源為尋求對中國經濟和政治發展軌跡進行權威分析和數據驅動見解的企業、政策制定者、投資者和研究人員提供服務。
