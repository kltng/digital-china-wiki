# China State Council Information Office White Papers (中国国务院白皮书)

**URL:** http://www.scio.gov.cn/zfbps

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

Official repository of white papers (白皮书) published by the State Council Information Office (SCIO) of the People's Republic of China. These government-issued policy documents provide authoritative statements on China's official positions across a wide range of topics including economic development, human rights, national defense, environmental protection, judicial reform, ethnic policies, foreign relations, and social governance. The white papers serve as primary sources for understanding Chinese government policy positions, official narratives, and policy priorities at particular historical moments. The collection spans from the early reform era to the present, enabling researchers to trace the evolution of Chinese policy discourse and official explanations of government actions.

## 摘要

中華人民共和國國務院新聞辦公室發布的白皮書官方庫。這些政府發布的政策文件提供中國政府在廣泛議題上的官方權威聲明，包括經濟發展、人權、國防、環境保護、司法改革、民族政策、外交關係和社會治理等。白皮書是了解中國政府政策立場、官方敘述和特定歷史時刻政策優先次序的主要來源。該收藏時間跨度從改革初期至今，使研究人員能夠追蹤中國政策話語的演進和對政府行動的官方解釋。
