# Digital Yingge Ceramics Museum

**URL:** https://digital.ceramics.ntpc.gov.tw/index.htm#page1

## Summary

The Digital Yingge Ceramics Museum is the online digital platform of the New Taipei City Yingge Ceramics Museum, Taiwan's premier museum dedicated to ceramic arts. The digital museum provides access to the museum's extensive collection of ceramic works, showcasing the rich history and cultural significance of ceramics in Taiwan and East Asia. As Yingge has been a major ceramic production center since the Qing dynasty, the digital platform serves as both a research resource and a public educational tool, documenting traditional ceramic techniques, contemporary ceramic art, and the cultural heritage of Taiwan's ceramic industry.

## 摘要

數位陶瓷博物館是新北市立鶯歌陶瓷博物館的線上數位平台。鶯歌是臺灣重要的陶瓷產地，自清代以來即為主要的陶瓷生產中心。該數位博物館提供對博物館豐富陶瓷藏品線上存取，展示了陶瓷藝術在臺灣及東亞的歷史與文化意義。平台作為研究資源與公眾教育工具，記錄了傳統陶瓷技藝、當代陶瓷藝術，以及臺灣陶瓷產業的文化遺產。
