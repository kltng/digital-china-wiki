# National Central Library (Taipei) 國家圖書館（臺北）

**URL:** https://aleweb.ncl.edu.tw/F

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The online catalog of Taiwan's National Central Library (國家圖書館) in Taipei, providing access to the library's comprehensive collections of books, journals, manuscripts, and special collections. Users can search the catalog to locate materials held by Taiwan's national library, including Chinese-language works, Taiwan studies materials, rare books, and government publications. The catalog facilitates resource discovery for researchers studying Taiwan, Chinese literature, and East Asian collections, offering bibliographic information and availability status for library holdings.

## 摘要

位於臺北的國家圖書館線上目錄，提供對該館藏圖書、期刊、手稿及特藏的廣泛收藏存取。使用者可搜尋目錄以定位臺灣國家圖書館所藏的中文著作、台灣研究資料、善本書及政府出版品。目錄促進研究台灣、中國文學及東亞典藏的研究人員發現資源，提供館藏的書目資訊與典藏狀態。
