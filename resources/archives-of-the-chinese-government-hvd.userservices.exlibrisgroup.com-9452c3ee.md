# Archives of the Chinese Government 中国政府资料库

**URL:** https://www.archives.gov.cn/

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A database providing access to official government archives and administrative documents from China. This resource collects and preserves government records, including official publications, administrative documents, policy papers, and other primary source materials produced by Chinese government agencies. The archive serves as a comprehensive repository for understanding the administrative history, policy development, and governmental activities of China. Materials may cover various periods of Chinese administrative history, with emphasis on contemporary government documents. The database enables researchers to access official government information for studies of Chinese politics, public administration, policy analysis, and government-civil society relations.

## 摘要

提供中國政府檔案和行政文件存取的資料庫。該資源收集和保存政府記錄，包括官方出版物、行政文件、政策文件以及中國政府機構產生的其他主要資料。該檔案作為了解中國行政歷史、政策發展和政府活動的綜合庫。材料可能涵蓋中國行政歷史的各個時期，重點為當代政府文件。該資料庫使研究人員能夠存取官方政府資訊，進行中國政治、公共行政、政策分析和政府與社會關係的研究。
