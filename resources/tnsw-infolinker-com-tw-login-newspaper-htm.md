# Central Daily News 中央日報 1928-2006 (Taiwan News Wisdom Network)

**URL:** http://tnsw.infolinker.com.tw/login_newspaper.htm?tpl=basic_search

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

Digitized archive of the Central Daily News (中央日報), spanning from 1928 to 2006, accessible through the Taiwan News Wisdom Network (台灣新聞智慧網). The Central Daily News was the official newspaper of the Kuomintang (KMT) and played a significant role in Republican China and later Taiwan's media landscape. This searchable database provides access to historical news coverage, editorials, and advertisements, offering valuable primary source material for researching 20th-century Chinese and Taiwanese history, politics, society, and culture. Access requires institutional subscription or IP authentication.
