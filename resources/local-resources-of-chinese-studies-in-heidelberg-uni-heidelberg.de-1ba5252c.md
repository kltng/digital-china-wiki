# Local Resources of Chinese Studies in Heidelberg

**URL:** http://www.uni-heidelberg.de/fakultaeten/philosophie/zo/boa/digital_resources/databases_en.html

## Summary

A resource page maintained by Heidelberg University providing information about Chinese studies resources available at the university and in the Heidelberg region. The page serves as a gateway to databases, digital collections, and academic materials related to Chinese studies accessible through Heidelberg's library system and East Asian studies programs. Resources may include bibliographic databases, full-text collections, digitized manuscripts, and specialized tools for Chinese language research. Heidelberg has a strong tradition in Sinology and East Asian studies, and this guide helps researchers navigate the university's offerings in Chinese language, literature, history, and philosophy.

## 摘要

海德堡大學維護的資源頁面，提供有關海德堡大學及海德堡地區可用的中文研究資源資訊。該頁面作為透過海德堡圖書館系統和東亞研究計畫可存取的中文研究資料庫、數位收藏和學術材料的入口。資源可能包括書目資料庫、全文收藏、數位化手稿以及中文研究專用工具。海德堡在漢學和東亞研究方面有深厚傳統，該指南幫助研究人員瀏覽大學在中文語言、文學、歷史和哲學方面的資源。
