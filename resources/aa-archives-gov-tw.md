# National Archives Information Network (Taiwan) 國家檔案資訊網

**URL:** https://aa.archives.gov.tw/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The National Archives Information Network (國家檔案資訊網) is Taiwan's official digital archives portal, managed by the National Development Council's Archives Administration (國家發展委員會檔案管理局). The platform provides online access to millions of digitized archival records, including government documents, historical records, and administrative materials spanning various periods of Taiwan's history. Users can search through extensive catalog holdings, view digitized materials, and apply for access to restricted records. The archive includes significant collections related to various historical events and administrative functions. The website features both basic and advanced search capabilities, browsing by category and chronological periods, and special collections highlighting important historical themes. Access to certain materials requires registration and application approval.

## 摘要

國家檔案資訊網是台灣的官方數位檔案入口網站，由國家發展委員會檔案管理局管理。該平台提供數百萬份數位化檔案記錄的線上存取，涵蓋政府文件、歷史檔案及各時期的行政資料。使用者可透過廣泛的目錄收藏進行搜尋、檢視數位化材料，並申請存取限制性檔案。檔案館收藏了與各歷史事件及行政功能相關的重要史料。網站提供基礎與進階搜尋功能、按類別與年代瀏覽，以及展示重要歷史主題的特藏。部分材料需註冊並經申請核准後方可存取。
