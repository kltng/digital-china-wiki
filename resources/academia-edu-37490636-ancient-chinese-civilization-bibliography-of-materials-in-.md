# Ancient Chinese Civilization: Bibliography of Materials in Western Languages

**URL:** https://www.academia.edu/37490636/Ancient_Chinese_Civilization_Bibliography_of_Materials_in_Western_Languages

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

A comprehensive bibliographic resource compiled by Dr. Lothar von Falkenhausen, listing Western-language scholarly materials related to ancient Chinese civilization. The bibliography encompasses works on Chinese archaeology, art history, religion, philosophy, language, and society from the Neolithic period through early imperial dynasties. It includes books, articles, monographs, and reference works published in European languages, providing researchers with access to international scholarship on pre-modern China. The bibliography is organized thematically and chronologically, covering topics such as ritual practices, religious systems, political structures, material culture, and textual traditions. This resource serves as an essential research tool for Sinologists, archaeologists, and historians seeking Western-language scholarship on ancient Chinese civilization.

## 摘要

由洛塔爾·馮·法爾肯豪森博士編纂的綜合性書目資源，匯列了有關中國古代文明的西文學術著作。該書目涵蓋了新石器時代至早期朝代的考古學、藝術史、宗教、哲學、語言及社會等方面的研究，包括以歐洲語言出版的書籍、文章、專著及參考工具書。該書目按主題與年代編排，涉及儀禮實踐、宗教體系、政治結構、物質文化及文本傳統等議題。此資源是漢學家、考古學家與歷史學者尋找中國古代文明西文學術研究的重要檢索工具。
