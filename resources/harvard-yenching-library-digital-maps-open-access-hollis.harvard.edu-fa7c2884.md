# Harvard-Yenching Library Digital Maps OPEN ACCESS

**URL:** https://hollis.harvard.edu/primo-explore/search?query=any%2Ccontains%2CHarvard-Yenching+Library+Digital+Maps%2CAND&tab=books&search_scope=default_scope&vid=HVD2&mode=advanced&offset=0

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A digital collection of historical maps of China and East Asia held by Harvard-Yenching Library at Harvard University. This open access collection provides digitized versions of rare and historically significant cartographic materials spanning various periods of Chinese and East Asian history. The maps document geographical knowledge, territorial boundaries, administrative divisions, and cultural regions across different dynasties and historical eras. Resources include traditional Chinese maps, military survey maps, urban planning maps, and thematic maps covering topics such as transportation, demographics, and economic activities. The collection serves as a primary source for historical geography, cartography, and spatial studies of China and East Asia, accessible through Harvard's library catalog system.
