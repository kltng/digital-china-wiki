# History of the Party Database 中共党史期刊数据库

**URL:** http://dangshi.dachengdata.com/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A specialized academic database focusing on the history of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP), collecting and digitizing party-related periodicals, historical documents, and scholarly publications. The database encompasses materials documenting CCP development from its founding through various historical periods, including revolutionary history, political campaigns, policy evolution, and leadership transitions. Resources include party journals, historical documents, memoirs, and academic research on CCP history. This database serves as a primary source for researchers studying Chinese political history, party organization, ideological development, and governance practices of the CCP.

## 摘要

專門關注中國共產黨歷史的學術資料庫，收集和數位化與黨相關的期刊、歷史文件和學術出版物。該資料庫包含記錄從中國共產黨成立到各個歷史時期發展的材料，包括革命歷史、政治運動、政策演變和領導層更替。資料包括黨刊、歷史文件、回憶錄和中國共產黨歷史的學術研究。該資料庫作為研究中國政治史、黨組織、意識形態發展和中國共產黨治理實踐的主要來源。
