# Data Science Services 哈佛定量社會科學分析中心IQSS

**URL:** https://dss.iq.harvard.edu/contact-us

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

Data Science Services provided by the Institute for Quantitative Social Science (IQSS) at Harvard University, offering support for computational and data-intensive research in the social sciences. The service provides consultation, training, and technical assistance in areas such as statistical computing, machine learning, text mining, geographic information systems, and data visualization. These services support researchers working with Chinese social science data, enabling advanced quantitative analysis of large datasets, computational text analysis of Chinese language materials, and spatial analysis of Chinese demographic and geographic data. IQSS facilitates cutting-edge research methodologies across social science disciplines.

## 摘要

哈佛大學定量社會科學研究所（IQSS）提供的數據科學服務，為社會科學中的計算和數據密集型研究提供支援。該服務提供統計計算、機器學習、文本挖掘、地理資訊系統和數據視覺化等領域的諮詢、培訓和技術協助。這些服務支援處理中國社會科學數據的研究人員，能夠對大型數據集進行進階定量分析、對中文材料進行計算文本分析，以及對中國人口和地理數據進行空間分析。IQSS 促進社會科學學科的前沿研究方法。
