# 國立故宮博物院圖書文獻數位典藏資料庫 (善本古籍-->明清輿圖)

**URL:** https://rbk-doc.npm.edu.tw/npmtpc/npmtpall?ID=32&SECU=1307740270&PAGE=rbmap%2Fmapdb%2Fsearch&ACTION=SC%2Cmapdb*%40%401820061207

**Language:** Chinese

## Summary

A digital database maintained by the National Palace Museum (Taipei) providing access to historical maps and geographical materials from the Ming and Qing dynasties. Part of the museum's extensive digitized rare book collection, this specialized repository contains yutu (輿图, imperial maps) documenting imperial territories, administrative divisions, military campaigns, urban layouts, and water management projects. The collection includes palace-produced maps, official survey maps, and cartographic works used for administrative purposes during China's late imperial period. These primary sources offer valuable insights into historical geography, imperial governance, territorial administration, and cartographic traditions of Ming and Qing China.

## 摘要

國立故宮博物院（台北）維護的數位資料庫，提供明清兩代歷史地圖和地理資料的存取。作為博物館廣泛數位化善本收藏的一部分，該專門資料庫包含記錄帝國疆域、行政區劃、軍事征戰、城市佈局和水利工程的輿圖。收藏包括宮廷製作地圖、官方測繪地圖和中國晚期帝國時期用於行政目的的地圖作品。這些主要資料為研究中國明清時期的歷史地理、帝國治理、領土管理和地圖傳統提供了珍貴見解。
