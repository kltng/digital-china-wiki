# Oxford Research Encyclopedia of Asian History

**URL:** https://asianhistory.oxfordre.com/

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

The Oxford Research Encyclopedia of Asian History is a comprehensive, peer-reviewed online reference work providing authoritative scholarly articles on Asian history from antiquity to the present. The encyclopedia covers all regions of Asia, with extensive coverage of Chinese history from ancient times through the contemporary period. Articles are written by leading scholars in the field and undergo rigorous peer review to ensure academic quality and reliability. Each entry provides an overview of key topics, historiographical debates, and suggestions for further reading. The platform is continuously updated with new articles and revisions to existing entries, reflecting the latest scholarship. The encyclopedia serves as an essential starting point for research on Asian and Chinese history, offering accessible yet scholarly content suitable for students, researchers, and general readers interested in Asian historical studies.
