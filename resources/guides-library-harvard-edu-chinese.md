## Summary

For questions or suggestions, please contact Xiaohe Ma, Chinese Studies Librarian, and/or Sharon Li-shiuan Yang, Librarian for Public Services and East Asian Digital Scholarship All of the photographs contained in the 28 albums assembled by Hedda Morrison and beaqueathed to Harvard-Yenching Library have been cataloged and digitized and can be viewed in VIA (Visual Information Access), the union catalog of visual resources at Harvard.
