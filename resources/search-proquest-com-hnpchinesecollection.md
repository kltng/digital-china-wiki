# ProQuest Historical Newspapers: Chinese Newspapers Collection (1832-1953)

**URL:** https://search.proquest.com/hnpchinesecollection?accountid=14026

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A ProQuest database collection digitizing historical Chinese-language newspapers published between 1832 and 1953, covering critical periods including the late Qing dynasty, the 1911 Revolution, and Republican China. This collection includes newspapers from major Chinese cities and overseas Chinese communities, providing firsthand coverage of political events, social transformations, economic developments, and cultural movements. The searchable full-text archive offers researchers access to contemporary reporting, editorials, advertisements, and opinion pieces, making it invaluable for studying modern Chinese history, Chinese diaspora communities, and the evolution of Chinese print media. Access requires institutional subscription through academic libraries.

## 摘要

ProQuest資料庫收藏，數位化1832年至1953年期間發行的歷史中文報紙，涵蓋清朝晚期、1911年革命和中華民國等關鍵時期。該收藏包括來自中國主要城市和海外華人社區的報紙，提供對政治事件、社會轉型、經濟發展和文化運動的第一手報導。可搜尋的全文檔案為研究人員提供對當時報導、社論、廣告和評論文章的存取，對研究現代中國歷史、海外華人社區和中文平面媒體的演進具有重要價值。存取需要通過學術圖書館的機構訂閱。
