# Pre-Republican Chinese books and manuscripts at the Yale University Library 美國耶魯大學圖書館中文古籍目錄

**URL:** http://search.library.yale.edu/catalog/14044401

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > yale

## Summary

A Yale University Library catalog record representing the library's holdings of pre-Republican Chinese books and manuscripts (中文古籍). This catalog entry encompasses the rich collection of Chinese rare books, manuscripts, and printed works dating from the imperial dynasties through the late Qing period (pre-1912). Yale's East Asian Library has built significant collections in Chinese studies, including works printed in traditional formats, woodblock editions, manuscripts, and early printed books. The collection covers classical literature, history, philosophy, religion, and local gazetteers, providing researchers access to primary sources for studying imperial Chinese civilization. The catalog record serves as an entry point to discover and request these materials for research.

## 摘要

耶魯大學圖書館目錄記錄，代表圖書館收藏的共和時期以前中文圖書和手稿（中文古籍）。該目錄記錄包含耶魯豐富的中國善本書、手稿和印刷作品收藏，年代從歷朝至晚清（1912年前）。耶魯東亞圖書館建立了重要的中文研究收藏，包括傳統格式印刷品、木刻版本、手稿和早期印刷書籍。收藏涵蓋古典文學、歷史、哲學、宗教和地方志，為研究人員提供研究中國古代文明原始資料的途徑。該目錄記錄是發現和請求這些資料進行研究的入門點。
