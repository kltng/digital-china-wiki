# Zhongguo wen shi zi liao ji cui 中国文史资料集粹

**URL:** https://chinamaxx.net/zt_wszl/home.jsp?dbNum=0

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

A digital collection of Chinese historical and literary materials (文史资料集粹) compiled from the People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC)文史资料 collections. This database contains firsthand accounts, memoirs, and historical documents covering modern Chinese history, with emphasis on political, military, economic, and cultural developments from the late Qing dynasty through the Republican era. Materials include personal recollections of historical events, biographical sketches, and documentation of local history. The collection serves as an important resource for researchers studying 20th-century Chinese history from the perspective of participants and eyewitnesses.

## 摘要

《中國文史資料集粹》是從中國人民政治協商會議文史資料系列編纂的數字化歷史文獻資料庫。此資料庫收錄了近代中國歷史的第一手資料、回憶錄和歷史文檔，重點涵蓋晚清至民國時期的政治、軍事、經濟和文化發展。資料包括歷史事件的個人回憶、人物傳記以及地方歷史文檔。該收藏為研究二十世紀中國歷史的學者提供了從參與者和目擊者視角出發的重要史料。
