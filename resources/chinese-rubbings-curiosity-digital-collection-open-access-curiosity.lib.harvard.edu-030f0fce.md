# Chinese Rubbings- CURIOSity Digital Collection 中文拓片 OPEN ACCESS

**URL:** https://curiosity.lib.harvard.edu/chinese-rubbings-collection

**Language:** Chinese

**Region:** China

## Summary

The Fine Arts Library and Harvard-Yenching Library holds rubbings of objects that date from the Qin Dynasty to the Ming Dynasty. Harvard is home to over 5,000 East Asian rubbings, the majority of which are from China. This joint collection contains some 2,700 rubbings from the Fine Arts Library and some 2,300 from Harvard-Yenching.
