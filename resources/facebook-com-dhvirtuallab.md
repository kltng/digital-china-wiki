# Digital Humanities Virtual Lab (Facebook)

**URL:** https://www.facebook.com/DHVirtualLab/

## Summary

The Digital Humanities Virtual Lab is a Facebook-based community page focused on digital humanities research and practice in Taiwan and the broader Chinese-speaking world. The page serves as a platform for sharing news, events, projects, and resources related to digital humanities, fostering connections among scholars, students, and practitioners. Through regular posts and community engagement, it helps disseminate information about digital tools, methodologies, and research projects while building a network of digital humanities researchers.

數位人文實驗室是一個基於 Facebook 的社群頁面，聚焦於臺灣及華語世界的數位人文研究與實踐。該頁面作為分享數位人文相關新聞、活動、專案與資源的平臺，促進學者、學生與從業人員之間的連結。透過定期發文與社群互動，協助傳播數位工具、研究方法與專案資訊，並建立數位人文研究人員的網絡。
