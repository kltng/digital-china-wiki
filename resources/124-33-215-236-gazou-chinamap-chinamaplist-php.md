# Toyo Bunko Image Database: Maps of the Chinese Empire and East Asia

**URL:** https://124.33.215.236/gazou/ChinaMap/ChinaMapList.php

**Language:** Chinese

**Discovered via:** LibGuides > stanford

## Summary

The Toyo Bunko (Oriental Library) Image Database provides open access to digitized historical maps of China and East Asia. This collection includes rare cartographic materials spanning several centuries, featuring maps created by European cartographers such as Nicolas Sanson, Herman Moll, Emanuel Bowen, and Martino Martini, as well as Chinese and Japanese mapmakers. The collection encompasses maps of the Chinese Empire, its provinces, Tartary, Korea, Japan, and surrounding regions. Materials include general maps of Asia, detailed provincial maps, city plans (including Beijing, Canton, Nanjing), and thematic maps documenting administrative divisions, territories, and geographical knowledge. This digital archive serves as a primary source for studying the historical cartography of East Asia, European representations of China, and the evolution of geographical knowledge during the early modern period.

## 摘要

東洋文庫圖像資料庫提供中國及東亞歷史地圖的開放存取。該收藏包含數世紀以來的珍貴地圖資料，特點是收錄了歐洲製圖師如尼古拉·桑松、赫爾曼·莫爾、伊曼紐爾·鮑恩和馬蒂諾·馬蒂尼等人創作的地圖，以及中國和日本地圖製作者的作品。收藏範圍涵蓋中國帝國及其省份、韃靼、朝鮮、日本和周邊地區的地圖。資料包括亞洲總圖、詳細省份地圖、城市平面圖（包括北京、廣州、南京），以及記錄行政區劃、領土和地理知識的主題地圖。該數位檔案是研究東亞歷史地圖學、歐洲對中國的表述以及近代早期地理知識演變的主要資料來源。
